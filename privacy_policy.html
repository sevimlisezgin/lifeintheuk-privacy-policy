Privacy Policy for Life in the UK Test Applications

Last updated: October 8, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Our applications ("Life in the UK Test" series including Turkish and Polish editions) do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. The apps operate entirely offline and do not require any personal data to function.

2. Data Storage

All test progress, scores, and user preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with any third parties, including us (the app developers).

3. No Internet Connection Required

The app functions completely offline. No internet connection is required for any of the app's features, and no data is transmitted over the internet.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. No cookies, tracking pixels, or similar technologies are employed.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any information from children or adults.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
